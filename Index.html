The Rise and Fall of Tommy

A picture-book tale of one man’s hilarious missteps

Tommy's Greatest Achievements

Tommy graduated college at 22, which is normal. He got his driver's license at 36, which is... not. He never had much luck with the ladies, but he tried his best.

Tommy the Spartan (For a Bit)

Tommy briefly played for the Spoto Spartans football team, but was removed after an incident involving cup-throwing at a gas station. Go Spartans!

Tommy’s Academic Journey

Let’s be honest... Tommy’s GPA barely existed. But hey — he showed up. That’s something, right?

0.9 GPA

Tommy’s Only Real Talent

If there's one thing Tommy could always do, it was carry heavy things. Boxes, couches, crates — you name it, he’ll lift it. Just don’t ask him to think too hard about it.

This storybook is dedicated to all the Tommys out there. Keep carrying stuff.
